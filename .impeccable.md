## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are friends hanging out in Discord calls at night, using the app as a shared second-screen party activity. Sessions are social, synchronous, and conversational rather than solitary or productivity-focused.

### Brand Personality
Warm, chill, geek.
The interface should feel inviting and playful, but not loud or chaotic. It should support a relaxed social vibe with clear readability during long evening sessions.

### Aesthetic Direction
Mostly dark theme, optimized for nighttime use.
Visual tone should blend atmospheric backgrounds and expressive but controlled motion:
- Borrow from Breakbot-style interaction polish and playful transitions.
- Borrow from Landon Norris and Dragonfly-style environmental background treatment (immersive, intentional, not flat).
- Avoid the polished-but-generic aesthetic exemplified by Nopturnia.
Overall direction: cozy competitive energy, not aggressive esports intensity.

### Design Principles
1. Social-first clarity: prioritize fast comprehension while people chat in real time.
2. Chill momentum: use motion to make actions feel alive, but keep pacing smooth and non-distracting.
3. Night-native comfort: dark surfaces with strong legibility and low eye strain over long sessions.
4. Playful trust: competition should feel fun and fair, with clear feedback for voting, host actions, and room state.
5. Distinctive atmosphere: backgrounds and transitions should be memorable enough to give the product identity, without sacrificing usability.
